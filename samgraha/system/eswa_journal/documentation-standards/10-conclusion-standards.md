## Purpose
Provide a concise, powerful closing statement that reinforces the validation without introducing new claims.

## Conclusion Summary
**Template:**
```markdown
[Restatement of the main contributions]
[Reinforcement of the empirical validation and results]
[Emphasis on applied relevance]
```

## Writing Guidance
- **Do:** Keep it concise (1-2 paragraphs). Tie back directly to the introduction's gap.
- **Don't:** Introduce any new claims, citations, or future work (future work belongs in the Limitations section).
