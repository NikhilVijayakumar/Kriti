## Purpose
Ensure the academic foundation of the paper meets Q1 journal standards.

## Reference Quality & Distribution
**Target:** 35–45 references.

**Distribution Requirements:**
- 60–70% recent (last 3-4 years).
- 20% foundational classics.
- 10–20% architecture/system design.
- 2–4 recent papers published in ESWA.

**Quality Requirements:**
- Q1/Q2 Scopus indexed journals.
- CORE A/A* conferences.

**Audit Rule:** 
- The presence of blog posts, non-peer-reviewed URLs, or known predatory journals triggers an immediate critical violation.
- All references cited in the text must appear in this section.
