## Purpose
Demonstrate scientific maturity by transparently acknowledging the boundaries and constraints of the proposed system.

## Limitations & Future Work
**Template:**
```markdown
[Scalability constraints]
[Domain dependence / Generalizability limits]
[Computational cost / Inference overhead]
[Data bias risks or Ethical considerations]
[Future work directions]
```

## Writing Guidance
- **Do:** Be radically transparent. Reviewers trust papers that know their own limits.
- **Don't:** Claim the system is perfect or universally applicable without evidence.
