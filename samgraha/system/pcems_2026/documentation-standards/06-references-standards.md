## Purpose

## Formatting Rules
**Rule**: 
- All references MUST be formatted in **APA style**.

## Typography Requirements
**Rule**: 
- Section heading must be Heading 1 (Arial, 12 pt bold).
- All reference items must be strictly in **8 pt font**.
