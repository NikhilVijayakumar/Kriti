## Purpose
Ensures strict compliance with the PCEMS 2026 title and author formatting guidelines.

## Title formatting
**Rule**: The title must be Arial Bold 14pt, centered.

## Author & Affiliation formatting
**Rule**: 
- Authors must be Arial 12pt Bold, centered (e.g., "Author A1, Author B2, and Author C3").
- Affiliations must be Arial 11pt, centered.
- E-mail of the corresponding author must be included.

## Keywords
**Rule**: Must have at least 4-5 keywords. The label "Keywords:" and the keywords themselves must be Arial 12pt bold.
