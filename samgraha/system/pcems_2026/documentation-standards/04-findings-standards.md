## Purpose
Presents the results of the research with a strict emphasis on correct table and media formatting as per PCEMS 2026.

## Content Requirements
**Template:**
```markdown
[Discuss the experimental findings and results]
```

## Media Placement Rules
**Rule**: 
- All images (illustrations, charts, photos) MUST be placed in the text **just after the first point of reference**. 
- Tables MUST be created using MS Word table functions and included **immediately after the first point of reference**.
- Tables and figures must NOT be placed at the end of the manuscript.

## Typography Requirements
**Rule**: 
- Section heading must be Heading 1 (Arial, 12 pt bold).
- Body text must be Arial 11 pt.
