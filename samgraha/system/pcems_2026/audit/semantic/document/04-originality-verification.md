# Originality Verification Audit

This section details the Originality Verification Audit.

## Version
1.0.0

## Engineering Intent
Enforces the exclusivity clause of PCEMS 2026 (the paper cannot be submitted elsewhere during the review period).

## Audit Objectives
- Perform a semantic cross-check of the abstract and title against recent pre-prints or concurrent conference proceedings to ensure no "substantially similar" content exists.

## Scoring Criteria

| ID | Weight | Score | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| C1 | mandatory | 0 or 100 | The manuscript content is unique and not detected in concurrent submissions or external databases |
