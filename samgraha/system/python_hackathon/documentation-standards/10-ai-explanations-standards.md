# 10. AI Explanations Standards

**Domain:** AI Explanations
**Audit Target:** SDK Dependencies, Prompt Templates, `README.md` (Semantic)

## Standard Definition
The automated AI text explanations produced by the hackathon submission must demonstrate contextual reasoning, high quality, and clarity to the end-user.

### Expected Evidence
1. **Tooling:** Inclusion of known LLM SDKs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, LangChain).
2. **Prompt Engineering:** Extraction of prompts into dedicated files or templates rather than deep hardcoding.
3. **Output Quality:** High clarity and strong contextual reasoning demonstrated in the final AI outputs.
